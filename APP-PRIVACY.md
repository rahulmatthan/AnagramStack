# Anagram Stack Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 15, 2026

Anagram Stack ("the app") is provided by Rahul Matthan.

## Summary

- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
- We do not require account sign-in to play.

## Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly from users through the app.

## Data Stored on Device

The app stores gameplay data locally on your device, including:

- chain progress
- completion times
- hint usage
- local app preferences

This local data is used only to provide game functionality (for example, resuming a game).

## Network Access

The app connects to GitHub-hosted content to download and refresh chain data used in gameplay.

- Source: public chain/manifest files
- Purpose: keep puzzle content up to date

No personal profile data is sent as part of gameplay.

## Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with data brokers or advertisers.

## Children

The app is a word puzzle game and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

## Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or data-related inquiries, please use:
